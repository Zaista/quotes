ABOUT QUOTES

Game of Quotes is fun and challenging puzzle game. Hidden before you is a famous quote. Try and guess it. You can see how many words the quote has, and how many letters each word contains. If you click on any of the letters, all the same letters in the quote will be highlighted as well. Type a letter to replace the highlighted underscores. No need to guess any punctuation signs. You have unlimited attempts. Good luck and have fun!
